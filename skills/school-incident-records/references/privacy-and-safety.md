# Privacy and Safety Rules

## Mask

Mask these in shared outputs:

- Student, guardian, teacher, staff, and school names
- Student numbers, class-number-name combinations, phone numbers, emails, addresses
- Online accounts, messenger IDs, social media IDs
- Medical, disability, family, financial, and disciplinary details not needed for the current record

Use `data/masking-map.teacher-only.md` only for teacher verification.

## Preserve

Do not edit:

- `raw/`
- `references/immutable-manuals/`

If source material contains harmful, emotional, or accusatory language, preserve it as source but summarize neutrally in outputs.

## Prohibited Wording

Avoid:

- "가해자", "피해자", "학폭", "교육활동침해", "괴롭힘", "고의", "징계 필요" as conclusions
- "확정됨", "판명됨", "처리 완료" unless the teacher provides an official source and asks for that source to be quoted as source status

Prefer:

- "학생A는 ...라고 진술함"
- "교사A의 관찰 기록에 따르면 ..."
- "현재 확인된 자료 기준"
- "확인 필요"
- "관리자 또는 학교 기준 확인 필요"

## Official Systems

Never:

- Log in to NEIS
- Enter records into NEIS
- Send reports
- Contact guardians or agencies
- File external reports
- Upload audio or documents to an external transcription service without explicit permission

Produce drafts and checklists only.
